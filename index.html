About SACE

Our Mission

Empower communities through education, human rights advocacy, health education and poverty alleviation for lasting positive change.

Our Vision

A world free of health and socio-economic injustices where every community thrives sustainably.

Philosophy

Youth & women are the foundation of socio-economic development driving change multi-dimensionally.

History

Founded in 2017, SACE has grown into a recognized force for community empowerment across Northern Uganda.

What We Do

Education Support

Scholarships, learning materials, and after-school mentorship for underprivileged children.

Health Awareness

Medical camps, hygiene campaigns, and disease prevention workshops in remote areas.

Youth Empowerment

Digital skills, entrepreneurship bootcamps, leadership training and job linkages.

Flagship Projects

School Infrastructure

Building classrooms and libraries in underserved communities, improving learning environments.

Community Cleanups

Eco-warriors program: recycling drives and sanitation campaigns for greener future.

Skills for Livelihoods

Tailoring, carpentry, and agribusiness training enabling economic independence.
