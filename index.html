About Us

Our Mission

To empower communities through education, youth development, and sustainable programs that create lasting positive change.

Our Vision

A world where every community has the resources and knowledge to thrive and create their own sustainable future.

Our History

Founded in 2015, SACE has grown from a small local initiative to a recognized community empowerment organization.

Our Services

Education Support

Providing educational resources, scholarships, and after-school programs to underprivileged children.

Health Awareness

Community health programs, free medical camps, and disease prevention education.

Youth Empowerment

Skills training, leadership development, and entrepreneurship programs for young people.

Our Projects

School Programs

Supporting local schools with infrastructure, learning materials, and teacher training.

Community Cleanups

Organizing regular environmental cleanups and waste management education.

Skills Training

Vocational training programs in carpentry, tailoring, computer skills, and more.
